Shout For Good

Charity Miles is a free app that empowers you to earn money for charities while walking, running or biking-- everyday.
Just choose a charity and hit the streets. As you walk, run or bike, the app will track your distance and you'll earn money for your charity: 10¢ per mile for bikers; 25¢ per mile for walkers and runners, all up to $1,000,000 courtesy of our corporate sponsors.
And this is just the beginning. As you walk, run or bike with Charity Miles, you also help us attract more sponsors to increase our sponsorship pool for everyone.
So please join the movement. And please invite your friends. Because changing the world is a team sport!

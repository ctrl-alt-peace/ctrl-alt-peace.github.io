Get Water!

Get Water! is a fun and challenging endless runner where kids learn about the impact of water scarcity through the story of Maya.
This educational game introduces kids to the water crisis and helps them think critically about the topic of water and its direct impact on education. Teach kids the importance of water and how to preserve it through this game and use the complementary free lesson plan in your classrooms, downloadable here: http://getwatergame.com/learning/
Get Water! is intended for 6-12 years old but is fun for all ages!
THE STORY OF MAYA

  • Travel with a girl named Maya on her quest for clean water!
  • Outwit mischievous monkeys, dodge flying turtles, and battle pesky peacocks!
  • The more water Maya can get, the faster she can get back to school; and the more she learns, the more efficiently she can get water!

FEATURES
  • Beautiful, kid-friendly easy-to-use interface!
  • Novel, intuitive touch-based gameplay!
  • Unlock 10 chapters in Maya's story by collecting water!
  • Important facts about water and tips on how to preserve it!
  • Activate your boomerang with a tap to fend off treacherous foes!
  • Acquire amazing skills from school to get more water!
  • Use special supplies to purify and protect your precious water!

Have fun helping Maya and charity: water bring clean water to developing countries! A portion of the proceeds will benefit charity: water, a non-profit organization dedicated to bringing clean water to developing nations!
